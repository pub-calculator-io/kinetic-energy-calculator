Instantly calculate kinetic energy, mass, or velocity using the KE = 1/2 mv² formula. Fast, free, and accurate Kinetic Energy Calculator for physics problems.

[https://www.calculator.io/kinetic-energy-calculator/](https://www.calculator.io/kinetic-energy-calculator/)

== Usage ==

Add the Kinetic Energy Calculator shortcode to your page, post or sidebar:

`[calcio_kinetic_energy_calculator]`

Add the following code to your template where you would like the Kinetic Energy Calculator to appear:

`<?php display_calcio_kinetic_energy_calculator(); ?>`

2. Activate the Kinetic Energy Calculator plugin through the "Plugins" menu in WordPress.
